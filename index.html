Split-screen speaking activity with 5 scenarios

Ready to Board?

This version is made for 2 students.

Put a piece of paper in the middle so Student A cannot see Student B’s side.

How to play

  • Student A and Student B each have different information.
  • They must ask and answer questions to complete the missing details.
  • Type the missing answers in the input boxes.
  • Click Check Answers to see if they are correct.
  • There are 5 scenarios.
🎫

Scene 1 · Boarding Pass Check

You're at the gate. A gate agent is checking everyone's boarding passes before they get on the plane.

✈ Gate Agent
"Good morning! Can I see your boarding pass and passport, please?"

What do you say?

💡 Vocabulary: "Here you go" = a polite way to hand something to someone. "One moment please" = asking for a few seconds. Both are very common and natural at airports.
💺

Scene 2 · Finding Your Seat

You're inside the plane. You can't find seat 24B. A flight attendant is nearby.

✈ Flight Attendant
"Hi there! Can I help you find your seat?"

What do you say?

💡 Vocabulary: "I'm looking for…" = I am trying to find… Row numbers go front to back (1→back). Seat letters: A/F = window, B/E = middle, C/D = aisle.
🧳

Scene 3 · Overhead Bin

Your bag is too big to lift alone. The overhead bin above your row is already full. A passenger nearby offers to help.

👨 Passenger
"Need a hand with that? The bin above us is full, but there's space a few rows back."

What do you say?

💡 Vocabulary: "Need a hand?" = Do you need help? (very common Australian phrase!) "That would be really helpful" = a warm, polite way to accept an offer. Overhead bin = the storage compartment above your seat.
🙋

Scene 4 · Your Seat Neighbour

You're in seat 24B (middle). The person in the aisle seat 24C needs to get past you to sit down.

👩 Neighbour
"Excuse me, I think that's my seat — 24C, the aisle one."

What do you say?

💡 Vocabulary: Both A and D are correct and polite! "Let me get up so you can get through" = very clear and helpful. "Go ahead!" = a quick, friendly way to invite someone to pass. "Excuse me" = used to politely get attention or pass someone.
🛟

Scene 5 · Safety Instructions

The plane is about to take off. A flight attendant asks everyone to prepare. Your tray table is still down.

✈ Flight Attendant
"Could you please fold your tray table up and make sure your seat belt is fastened? We're about to take off."

What do you say?

💡 Vocabulary: Both A and C are correct! "Sure thing!" = Yes, absolutely! (very Australian/casual 🇦🇺) "Of course, sorry about that" = polite and apologetic. Tray table = the small fold-down table in front of you. Fasten your seatbelt = click/lock your seatbelt.
🏆

Well done!

You successfully boarded the plane!

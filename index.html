✈ Boarding the Plane

5 Split-Screen Roleplay Scenarios

Conversation 1 of 5

Classroom Roleplay Activity

This version is designed for two students. Put a piece of paper in the middle so Student A cannot see Student B's side, and Student B cannot see Student A's side.

Students must ask and answer questions to get the missing information, then type the answers into the boxes.

  • Student A and Student B each have different information.
  • They must speak in English to complete the task.
  • Click Check Answers to see if the missing information is correct.
  • There are 5 scenarios, like your Boarding the Plane game.
